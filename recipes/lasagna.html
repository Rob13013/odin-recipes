Lasagna

A Perfectly Baked Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 500g Pork Mince
  • Italian Herbs
  • 250g Cheddar Cheese Grated
  • 500g Mozzarella Cheese Grated
  • 420g Tomato Puree
  • 1 Bottle Bechemel Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste

Preparation Method

In a non-stick pot:
  1. Fry the tomato puree for 10 minutes, stirring constantly
  2. Add the Mince and fry until Golden Brown
  3. Add the Italian Herbs and Salt & Pepper to taste
  4. Add the Cheddar Cheese and Bechemel Sauce
  5. Cook for 15 Minutes on low heat

  6. Spray a glass pyrex dish with spray & cook
  7. Ladle just enough mince to cover the bottom of the pyrex dish
  8. Sprinkle with some Mozzarella Cheese
  9. Cover with a layer of lasagna sheets
  10. Repeat steps 7 - 9 until the dish is full
  11. Ladle a layer of mince on the last layer
  12. Spread the last of the Mozzarella chees on top

  13. Bake at 180Â°C for 45 minutes
  14. Remove from oven and allow to cool and set for about 15 minutes
  15. Serve with fresh garlic rolls
